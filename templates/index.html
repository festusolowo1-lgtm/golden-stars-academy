Our Story

Founded on Faith, Driven by Excellence

Founded in September 2011 by visionary educationists Daniel Ayo David and his wife, Golden Stars Academy was built from nearly two decades of hands-on experience nurturing young minds.

Driven by faith and a deep commitment to holistic development, they created a school where academic rigour meets character formation — a place where every child is seen, valued, and empowered.
